Emotional Intelligence


What is Emotional Intelligence?

Aka EQ is the ability to understand, use and manage your own emotions in positive ways to relieve stress, communicate effectively, empathize with others, overcome challenges and avoid conflict.

How is it different from IQ?

IQ is your ability to solve problems, use logic, grasp and communicate complex ideas. EQ is your ability to recognize emotion in yourself and others, and to use that awareness to guide your decisions.

Why is it important?

IQ is not the only determinant of life success, EQ is as important. Nowadays, employers do not only look for technical skills but soft skills as well, like being able to work coherently in a team.
